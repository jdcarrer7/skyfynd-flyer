Skyfynd

Operating Agreement

A California Limited Liability Company

This Operating Agreement (the "Agreement") is entered into as of February 11, 2026 (the "Effective Date"), by and between Juan Carrero and Carlos Carrero (collectively referred to as the "Members").

Article I: Company Formation

1.1 Formation. The Members acknowledge that the Limited Liability Company ("Company") named Skyfynd LLC was formed under the laws of the State of California on 05/21/2025.

1.2 Entity Number. The Secretary of State Entity Number for the Company is B20250131189.

1.3 Principal Office. The principal office of the Company shall be located at: 2108 N ST STE N, Sacramento, CA 95816.

1.4 Registered Agent. The Registered Agent for the Company is Northwest Registered Agent, Inc.

Article II: Business Purpose

2.1 Purpose. The specific business purpose of the Company is to operate as a boutique software and digital services company specializing in modern website design, application development, UI/UX, and brand-integrated digital experiences for small businesses, startups, and independent creatives. The Company may also engage in any other lawful business activity allowed under California law.

Article III: Members, Contributions & Ownership

3.1 Members & Percentage Interest. The Members and their ownership percentages are as follows:

Name Ownership %
Juan Carrero 50%
Carlos Carrero 50%

3.2 Liability. No Member shall be personally liable for the debts, obligations, or liabilities of the Company solely by reason of being a Member.

Article IV: Management

4.1 Management Structure. The Company shall be Member-Managed, consistent with the Articles of Organization filed with the California Secretary of State.

4.2 Authority. Both Members have the authority to bind the Company in contracts, manage daily operations, and make business decisions.

4.3 Voting. All major decisions (including but not limited to: loans, major purchases over $5,000, selling company assets, or dissolving the Company) require a unanimous vote (100%) of the Members.

Article V: Profit, Loss & Distributions

5.1 Allocation. Net profits and net losses shall be allocated to the Members in proportion to their Ownership Percentage (50/50).

5.2 Distributions. Distributions of cash or other assets shall be made at such times and in such amounts as the Members unanimously agree.

Article VI: Deadlock & Dispute Resolution

6.1 Deadlock. In the event the Members are equally divided on a material business decision ("Deadlock"), the Members agree to the following procedure:

  1. Good Faith Negotiation: The Members shall first attempt to resolve the issue through direct negotiation for a period of 14 days.
  2. Mediation: If negotiation fails, the Members agree to hire a neutral third-party mediator to facilitate a resolution.
  3. Tie-Breaker: If mediation fails, the Members agree to appoint a mutually trusted advisor (e.g., the Company CPA or Attorney) to cast a deciding vote to resolve the deadlock.

Article VII: Transfer of Interest

7.1 Restrictions. No Member may sell, assign, or transfer their interest in the Company to a third party without the written consent of the other Member.

7.2 Right of First Refusal. If a Member receives a bona fide offer to purchase their interest, they must first offer it to the other Member at the same price and terms. The remaining Member has 30 days to accept or reject the offer.

Article VIII: Dissolution

8.1 Events of Dissolution. The Company shall be dissolved upon:

  1. The unanimous written agreement of all Members.
  2. The entry of a decree of judicial dissolution under California law.
